Treatments

At AnjioCare Heart & Vascular Institute, we offer a comprehensive range of treatments tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient. Our advanced therapies include:

  • Angioplasty and Stenting: Minimally invasive procedures to open blocked arteries and restore blood flow.
  • Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG): Surgical intervention to improve blood flow to the heart.
  • Heart Valve Repair and Replacement: Procedures to address damaged or dysfunctional heart valves.
  • Arrhythmia Management: Advanced techniques including ablation and pacemaker implantation to regulate heart rhythms.
  • Heart Failure Management: Comprehensive care plans including medication, lifestyle changes, and device therapy.

Our multidisciplinary team works closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans that prioritize their health and well-being.

EQUIPMENTS

Cath lab

Our state-of-the-art Cath Lab is equipped with the latest technology to provide advanced diagnostic and interventional cardiology services. It features high-resolution imaging systems, 3D mapping, and real-time monitoring capabilities, allowing our specialists to perform complex procedures with precision and safety. The Cath Lab is designed to enhance patient comfort and streamline workflow, ensuring efficient and effective care for individuals with cardiovascular conditions.

Imaging Technology

We utilize cutting-edge imaging technology, including 3D echocardiography, cardiac MRI, and CT angiography, to obtain detailed views of the heart and vascular system. These advanced imaging modalities enable accurate diagnosis, treatment planning, and monitoring of heart diseases, enhancing our ability to deliver personalized care.

Intra Vascular Ultrasound

Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) is a cutting-edge imaging technology used in our Cath Lab to provide detailed, real-time images of the inside of blood vessels. This advanced technique allows our cardiologists to assess the extent of arterial blockages, evaluate plaque characteristics, and guide interventional procedures with greater precision. By utilizing IVUS, we enhance the accuracy of diagnoses and improve treatment outcomes for our patients with cardiovascular conditions.

Optical Coherence Tomography

Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) is an advanced imaging technology employed in our Cath Lab to obtain high-resolution, cross-sectional images of blood vessels. This technique uses light waves to capture detailed images of the vessel walls, allowing our cardiologists to assess plaque morphology, stent placement, and vessel healing with exceptional clarity. OCT enhances our ability to make informed decisions during interventional procedures, ultimately improving patient outcomes in cardiovascular care.

Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump

The Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump (IABP) is a mechanical device used in our Cath Lab to support patients with severe heart conditions. It consists of a balloon that is inserted into the aorta and inflates and deflates in sync with the cardiac cycle. This action helps to increase blood flow to the coronary arteries and reduce the workload on the heart, improving oxygen delivery to vital organs. The IABP is particularly beneficial for patients experiencing cardiogenic shock or undergoing high-risk cardiac procedures, providing critical support during recovery.

Management of Calcified Coronaries

At AnjioCare Heart & Vascular Institute, we specialize in the management of calcified coronary arteries using advanced techniques and technologies. Our approach includes the use of intravascular lithotripsy (IVL) to safely fracture calcium deposits, allowing for optimal stent placement and improved blood flow. We also employ rotational atherectomy and specialized balloon angioplasty to effectively treat heavily calcified lesions. Our experienced team tailors treatment plans to each patient's unique condition, ensuring the best possible outcomes in the management of calcified coronary disease.

Rota Pro-Boston scientific

The Rota Pro system by Boston Scientific is an advanced rotational atherectomy device used in our Cath Lab to treat heavily calcified coronary arteries. This innovative technology utilizes a high-speed rotating burr to precisely remove calcium deposits from the arterial walls, facilitating optimal stent placement and improving blood flow. The Rota Pro system is designed to enhance procedural safety and efficacy, allowing our cardiologists to effectively manage complex coronary lesions and improve patient outcomes in cardiovascular care.

Intravascular lithotripsy

Intravascular Lithotripsy (IVL) is a cutting-edge technology used in our Cath Lab to treat calcified coronary arteries. This innovative technique employs sonic pressure waves to fracture calcium deposits within the arterial walls, allowing for improved vessel compliance and optimal stent deployment. IVL is minimally invasive and enhances procedural safety, making it an effective option for patients with heavily calcified lesions. By utilizing IVL, we aim to improve blood flow and overall cardiovascular health for our patients.

Cardiac health Checkup

Our comprehensive Cardiac Health Checkup is designed to assess your heart health and identify potential risk factors for cardiovascular disease. The checkup includes a series of tests such as blood pressure measurement, cholesterol and lipid profile analysis, electrocardiogram (ECG), echocardiogram, and stress testing. Our experienced cardiologists will evaluate the results and provide personalized recommendations for lifestyle modifications, preventive measures, and treatment options to help you maintain optimal heart health.
